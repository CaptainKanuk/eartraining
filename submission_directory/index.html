Musicans, composers, and students of music theory have a great need to train their ear and musicianship skills, yet the software available to use for these purposes is outdated, inaccessible, and unusable. Mezzo attempts to change this by offering an interactive, fun, and beautiful approach to ear training. Using a modern interface and progressive games, Mezzo is fun and easy to use for musicans and non-musicians alike.

Access Mezzo at http://ear-training.herokuapp.com

Contact PM Delaney Granizo-Mackenzie at dgranizo@.
